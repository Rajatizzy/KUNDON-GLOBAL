About us

Kundan Global is the marketing division of Microsoft Nonwovens, a large Indian enterprise dealing in all kinds of shirt Interlinings , coat interlinings, Cond Water soluble embroidery backing for Schiffli and Multihead machines and all kinds of Nonwovens over the past 22 years.

We started as an import house and today are one of the leading manufacturer, importer and distributor of all nonwoven products throughout India. We have our factories and offices in Delhi, Gurgaon and Bhiwadi. We have invested in 5 different manufacturing lines for various nonwovens products which include, Felt, PV/Chemical Bond Interlining, Thermobond Interlining, Embroidery Backing among others. We own the "KingBond" brand of interlinings. Dealer Network: The company has a vast network of whole sellers and distributors throughout India , with the main thrust in Mumbai, Banglore, Chennai , Tirpur, Ludhiana and Delhi.
