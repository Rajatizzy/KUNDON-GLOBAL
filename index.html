Kundan Global, Inc. is the go to company for textile accessories such as fusible and non-fusible nonwoven interlinings, Embroidery backing (Cold water soluble film and Random paper for multi-head, Chemical paper for Schiffli machines) TPU tape among others. The company branched out of 'Microsoft Nonwovens' which was established in 1994, one of the leading manufacturers & importers of interlining & embroidery backing.

Our Infrastructure

We have factories in Sohna & Bhiwadi manufacturing embroidery backing tear away (22-60 gsm dimensions as per your requirements).

We import around 10 containers of various interlinings every month which are warehoused in our and warehouses located in Okhla and Udyog Vihar, which enables us to fulfill orders in and around NCR including Manesar with ease.

We have Pan–India presence with offices in Bangalore, Mumbai, Tirpur, Surat and Ludhiana.
